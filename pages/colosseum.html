Mission:

The goal of the Colosseum project is to implement a platform that lets users express their full creativity and share it in truly immersive ways.

    Key Features:

  • User created themes, avatars, environs to populate their "rooms" at unique URLs.
  • Modular and user oriented, code-optional setup, with a custom dev-mode for programmers.
  • Endless integration possibilities. Connect your favorite code editor and bend cyberspace.
